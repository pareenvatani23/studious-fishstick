# TrueShift — store listing & ASO (draft)

Positioning is anchored to the keyword research (Google Autocomplete + SERP):
lead with **reframe negative thoughts / stop overthinking / CBT**, NOT "daily
reset" ("daily reset" autocompletes to *games* — FFXIV/WoW/Genshin). This also
matches our best-evidenced mechanism (reframe + one small action).

## App name / subtitle
- **Name:** TrueShift
- **Subtitle (iOS, ≤30 chars):** `Reframe & stop overthinking`
- **Short line:** Reframe negative thoughts & stop overthinking — a calm 3-minute CBT reset.

## App Store keyword field (iOS, 100 chars, comma-sep, no spaces)
```
reframe,negative,thoughts,overthinking,cbt,cognitive,reframing,thought,record,calm,anxiety,racing,mind,reset
```

## Google Play — short description (≤80 chars)
`Reframe negative thoughts & stop overthinking with a calm 3-minute CBT reset.`

## Google Play — long description (keywords woven naturally)
> **Feeling pulled by a thought you can't shake?** TrueShift is a calm, private,
> CBT-informed reset that helps you **reframe negative thoughts** and **stop
> overthinking** in about three minutes.
>
> Tell it what happened, get a kinder, more balanced way to see it (cognitive
> reframing), and one small step to take. A gentle voice can read it to you.
>
> - **Reframe negative thoughts** with guided cognitive reframing
> - **Stop overthinking** with a short, in-the-moment reset
> - One small, doable action every time (behavioural activation)
> - Private by default — no account, stored on your device
> - No streaks, no shame, no endless feed
>
> TrueShift is a self-help tool, not therapy or a crisis service.

## Target keyword clusters (priority order)
| Priority | Cluster | Why (demand / competition) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | reframe negative thoughts (+ examples/how to) | High demand, **low app competition** (SERP = blogs) |
| 2 | stop overthinking (app) | Very high demand, medium (indie) competition, exact-match to our reset |
| 3 | cognitive reframing / restructuring (app) | High intent, medium competition |
| 4 | CBT app / free CBT app / CBT app for anxiety | High app-intent, medium competition |
| 5 | thought journal / thought record app | Low competition long-tail (we have journaling) |
| — | calm anxiety / anxiety app | Very high demand but **saturated** (Calm/Headspace) — secondary only |

## Content/SEO (if a marketing site is added)
Write for the low-competition informational long-tails and funnel to the app:
- "How to reframe negative thoughts (with examples)"
- "Cognitive reframing worksheet & examples"
- "How to stop overthinking — a 3-minute method"

## Avoid
- Leading with "daily reset" (gaming SERP).
- Competing head-on for "calm" / "anxiety app" (owned by Calm/Headspace).

> Volumes/difficulty here are directional (free tools: autocomplete + SERP).

> priority list before finalising the ASO field.
